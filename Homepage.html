A Zondle-esque studying experince, specifically designed for my LA class

When Zondle went down I decided to build this website with these games for my classmates to study as usual

To Study

When my teacher announced that Zondle wouldn't be available after this test, I decided to make a parody of Zondle.
Zondle was very affective for me and my classmates to study with, especially for our Word of the Day Quizzes. The games
aren't generally very visually pleasing so I didn't think it would be very hard. I also thought that it was likely that I could
find the games source code for free so it would be especially easy to make some of the games. I am starting out with making
popular "Dodge the meteors in a spaceship" first, for a couple reasons: It is very popular; It doesn't use complicated
images or movement; and finally it would be relatively easy to recreate. To be completely honest I will admit part of the
reason I am so intent on making this is because it will look great in a portfolio.
